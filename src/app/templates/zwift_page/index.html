Why ?

The popular training software Zwift is becoming more and more popular !

So is customised training with Zwift and all of the features, one of them being the custom workout section in which you can create and ride your own workouts.

Unfortunatly that 'creator' is not user friendly and long or complex workouts take hours to build, thats why we are here.

We provide you with a quick Copy and Paste friendly way to create more complex workouts and download them. 

How ?

In the  legend down below are the possible blocks you may enter in to the Generator field. Enter your numbers and click on 'Show me my workout!' to see what your current workout looks like!

You should try the 'Example' !

When you are finished click 'Generate my .zwo workout file' and import it into Zwift (Explanation) , note that a pop up window might get blocked by your browser, to receive the generated workout you should open that window.

FAQ

Q: One of the blocks I defined is not shown in the graph
A:  You should check if the spelling aligns with the legend and you did not miss any closing bracket or so, in the  text block you must also use the ' correctly. If the problem persists please send an email including a screenshot.

Q: Nothing happens when i click generate workout
A:  Your browser might block the pop up window in which the download is started (usually seen in the adress bar), you should try to allow that pop up.

Q: I cannot use the site on my mobile device
A:  Unfortunatly we do not support mobile device use at the moment, sorry !
